About ServiceLK

Who We Are

ServiceLK is your reliable platform connecting you to trusted home service professionals across Sri Lanka. From plumbing, electrical work, cleaning services, to handyman tasks, we make finding help easy and safe.

Our mission is to provide efficient, quality, and affordable services to every household in Sri Lanka, while empowering local professionals with opportunities to grow their businesses.

Our Mission

To simplify home maintenance and repair for every Sri Lankan household by providing a trusted and seamless service platform.

Our Vision

To be Sri Lanka’s most trusted and user-friendly home service marketplace, connecting professionals and customers with confidence.

Our Core Values

  • Quality Service
  • Customer Satisfaction
  • Professional Growth
  • Transparency & Integrity
